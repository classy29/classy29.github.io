n week 3 we are introduced computer aided design (CAD). CAD is divided in 2 parts, 2d design and 3d design. As per name, 2d design is something like drawings we do on canvas and drafts which are made in engineering drawings. Its only based on 2 dimensions. in CAD we do 2d drawings in computers. 3d design is one step ahead, itâ€™s the use of computers to make the 3-dimensional prototypes. We can use those models to ideation and prototyping(simulation).

I also wanted to research on some key ideas about 3d design which are Generative design concept and script-based cad software (software which uses scripting instead of different features to do different operations).

Generative design is research area in CAD which helps the designer to ideate in better way, it takes information of a prototype like load constrains, types of loads and operating conditions and presents different models which are able to satisfy requirements. you can get more information here.

Concept of generative design from webpage



Script-based cad software are very fast when you have to so number of jobs in very less amount of time, traditional (history-based) softwares are very efficient for this type of jobs, because they work on history tree and take more re-computation time.
